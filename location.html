Bandung

Bandung is the capital city of West Java province in Indonesia and is known as "Kota Kembang" (City of Flowers) or "Parijs van Java" (Paris of Java) due to its lush greenery and European-style colonial architecture. It's a vibrant city with a rich history, known for its fashion, culinary scene, and creative industry.
